About Me

Hi, thanks for visiting my portfolio. I’m UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer from Jawalakhel, Nepal. I started my professional career back in 2014. Since then I had the pleasure working with number of clients, and much more projects, ranging from small single page experiments to large web projects. Usually I would stray into interactive design. At work I’m a passionate perfectionist who loves healthy competition and challenges. If I’m not at work I’m probably playing games, watching movies, riding motorcycles or imagining I know how to play guitar. I am happy I can share samples of my work with you, and I would be delighted to answer any questions you may have.
